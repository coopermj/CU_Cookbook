BBQ Chicken Sandwich
Prep Time: 10min | Cook Time: 5min | Difficulty: *

Ingredients

2 chicken breasts
1 sub roll (or hamburger bun)
1/4 plate of onions
1/4 plate of green peppers
2 slices of cheese (pepperjack is best)
1 large bowl of BBQ sauce
Butter
BBQ Seasoning


Seasoning

40% Western BBQ Seasoning
40% Mesquite Herb Seasoning
10% Garlic Powder
10% Chile Pepper Seasoning


Directions

1) Get a sub roll from the sub line and butter the inside of it.

2) Dice up chicken into bite-size pieces.

3) Open the sub roll and put face down on the grill so the buttered side gets toasted.

4) Put chicken, onions, and peppers onto the grill. Dice up the peppers and onions once they are on the grill.

5) Once the chicken and veggies are lightly grilled (chicken should be slightly darkened on the outside) sprinkle BBQ seasoning and mix together.

6) Pour the bowl of BBQ sauce over everything and mix until evenly distributed.

7) Sprinkle some salt and pepper over everything and mix together.

8) Take sub roll of the grill and put on a blue plate.

9) Lay cheese on the inside of the sub roll and then scoop the BBQ chicken off the grill and into the sub roll.

10) Clean off the grill for the next person to use.

11) Enjoy!

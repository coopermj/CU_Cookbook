Chicken Caesar Wrap
Prep Time: 10min | Cook Time: 10min | Difficulty: **

Ingredients

2 chicken breasts
1 tortilla/wrap
1/2 small bowl of croutons
1/2 plate of lettuce
1/4 plate of onions
3/4 large bowl of Caesar salad dressing
1/2 small bowl of parmesan cheese
Caesar seasoning


Seasoning

40% Italian Seasoning
35% Lemon Pepper Seasoning
15% Onion Powder
10% Garlic Powder


Directions

1) Collect all ingredients and dice chicken into bite-size pieces.

2) Squeeze some oil onto the grill and put on the chicken and onions and let cook until lightly grilled.

3) Sprinkle the Caesar seasoning over everything, along with a little salt and pepper as well, and mix.

4) Pour the bowl of Caesar salad dressing on everything and mix together.

5) Scoop chicken and onions off of the grill and place in a bowl with the croutons, lettuce, and parmesan cheese. Take another bowl and place over the first bowl, and shake until the Caesar dressing is mixed in with the lettuce and everything else uniformly.

6) Pour everything from the bowl onto a tortilla and roll.

7) Clean the grill for the next person to use.

8) Enjoy!
